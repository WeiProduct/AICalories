Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

AI Calories ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Profile information (age, gender, height, weight, activity level)
  • Dietary goals and preferences
  • Food intake records and meal photos
  • Water intake data
  • Health and fitness goals

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized nutrition tracking and recommendations
  • Analyze food images using AI technology
  • Track your progress towards health goals
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Send you reminders and notifications (with your permission)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally backed up to iCloud if you have enabled iCloud backup. We do not store your personal data on our servers. Food images are processed using AI technology, but the images themselves are not retained after processing.

Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI's API for food recognition and nutritional analysis. When you take a photo of food, the image is sent to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy. We do not share any other personal information with third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of notifications

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@aicalories.app
